SpaceX


SpaceX designs, manufactors and lauches the worlds most advanced rockets using the most inivative rockets the worlds has seen. The company was founded by Elon Musk in 2002 for the purpose of revolutionising space travel. The company currently employs over 5000 people including many of the worlds best aerospace engineers.


History of SpaceX

As of December 2010, SpaceX became the first private company to launch and return a spacecraft from low earth orbit. In May 2012, they sent the Dragon spacecraft to the International Space Station becoming the first private company to do so. On the 21st of December 2015, SpaceX became the first to land a rocket stage safely back on earth to be used again in the future. On April 8, 2016 The Falcon 9 rocket landed safely upon a droneship in the middle of the ocean.

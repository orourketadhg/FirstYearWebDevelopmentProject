SpaceX Headquarters

The SpaceX Headquarters are located in Hawthorne, California USA. Originally built by the Northrop Corportaion, who built Boeings Boeing 474's fuselages, This location surves as a office and factory. This location is where the SpaceX company builds its various rocket engine, spacecraft and stages.

The Space Exploration Technologies corp. is the worlds leading aerospace and space transport company. They have single handly pushed the technology we use to travel into space into the next level. By the hands of their founder Elon Musk they aim to push the technology we use to bring both people and resourses into space, travel to different planets and create a better ways to travel the cosmos.

  2. X.com and PayPal

  4. Tesla
  5. Hyperloop

  7. The Boring Company

Each one of these companies has been the start of a revolutionary idea. From the inovative electric cars of Tesla to the creation of OpenAI, a artificial intelligence company who wants to make AI in a safe and beneficial way to humanity.
